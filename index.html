ABOUT JRASSETS JR Assets has set out to become one of the largest and most trusted traders in a vast array of commodities and raw materials to industrial consumers and wholesale customers. Our goal is to be a reliable and competitive business partner in the markets which we serve with a customer service orientation. We realize that we play a vital role in the value chain of our customers and seek to ensure that we help their businesses run as efficiently as possible.
The products we trade originate from an exclusive list of trusted and vetted sources, some of which are some of the largest suppliers in the world. Our customers, like us, are global players that are in the agricultural, energy, construction, government, and fast moving consumer goods (FMCG) industries.
JR Assets trading is a privately held company owned largely by its management and employees. Headquartered in Madison, Wisconson we are a lean organization that prides itself on productivity and efficiency.
